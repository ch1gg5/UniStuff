## 💡 Revision Tips

- Break revision into **timed sessions** (e.g. Pomodoro: 25 min on / 5 min off).
- Summarise topics in your **own words** — if you can't explain it simply, revisit it.
- Practice past papers **under exam conditions** (timed, no notes).
- Use the `Notes/` summaries as a **quick reference** the night before an exam.
